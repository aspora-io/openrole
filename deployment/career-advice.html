Interview Preparation

Common Interview Questions

"Why do you want to work here?"

Research the company's values, recent news, and culture. Align your answer with their mission and explain how you can contribute.

"What are your strengths and weaknesses?"

Choose strengths relevant to the role. For weaknesses, mention something genuine but show how you're working to improve it.

"Where do you see yourself in 5 years?"

Show ambition while being realistic. Focus on skills you want to develop and how you plan to grow within the company.

"Why are you leaving your current job?"

Stay positive. Focus on what you're looking for in your next role rather than what's wrong with your current situation.

The STAR Method

Use this framework to structure your answers to behavioral questions:

S - Situation

Set the context for your story

T - Task

Describe what needed to be done

A - Action

Explain what you did

R - Result

Share the outcome and what you learned
