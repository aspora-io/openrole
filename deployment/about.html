About OpenRole

We're revolutionizing the job market by eliminating ghost jobs and requiring salary transparency from every employer.

Our Mission

Verified Employers

Every employer is verified through Companies House data. No fake companies, no scams - just legitimate opportunities.

Salary Transparency

All job postings must include salary ranges. No more wasting time on applications only to find the pay doesn't match your expectations.

No Ghost Jobs

We actively monitor and remove inactive listings. Every job on our platform represents a real opportunity with active hiring.

Our Story

OpenRole was born out of frustration with the current state of job hunting. Too many job seekers waste countless hours applying for positions that don't exist, or discovering after multiple interview rounds that the salary doesn't meet their basic needs.

We believe that transparency is the foundation of a fair job market. By requiring all employers to display salary ranges and verifying their legitimacy through official government data, we're creating a platform where both job seekers and employers can make informed decisions.

Our platform currently features 345 verified companies from the UK's Companies House registry, offering over 125 active job opportunities across various industries. Every employer has been validated, and every job posting includes transparent salary information.
